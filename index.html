Description of the Exam and type of Questions

  • The examination mainly tests whether you actually had 1+ years of experience in AWS as it expects. Most of the questions combine multiple AWS services.
  • The questions involve real time scenarios where you are your ability to provide a highly available,scalable, fault tolerant website.
  • Sample Question: In a virtual Private Network, with a public and private subnet, where would you deploy load balancers, web servers and database servers, for higher security.
  • A lot fo questions are aimed at your ability to migrate a client's on premise infrastructure to AWS as per his requirement and suggesting best practices in security.
  • This exam has two versions SAA C01 and SAA C02. SAA C01 is valid until July 1st. It is the retiring version and has all the core AWS concepts. SAA C02 is the basically C01 with additional concepts (HPC, Active Directory Integration and more).
  • To clarify, SAA C01, is still valid, and while the exam can only be taken until July 1st 2020, the certification lasts for 3 years from the time you took the exam. The common misconception is that the certificate itself expires on the 1st of July which is not true.
  • Writing the SAA C01 examination covers all the core AWS services ( most of them at least), so that you can suggest solutions at an associate level and make intelligent decisions for the deployment of a product. It is challenging since there is a lot of services to learn.
